Chicken Manchow

Chicken Manchow Soup is a popular Indo-Chinese dish known for its spicy and tangy flavors.

  • Ingredients:

  • 250 grams boneless chicken, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped vegetables (carrots, cabbage, capsicum, beans)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped garlic
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth or stock
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • tablespoon cornstarch (cornflour), dissolved in water
    • 1
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish

    Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot or wok over medium heat. Add garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped onions and cook until they become translucent.
  3. Add the finely chopped chicken and stir-fry until the chicken turns white and is cooked through.
  4. Add the finely chopped vegetables and cook for a few minutes until they become slightly tender.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Stir well and then pour this mixture into the pot.
  6. Stir everything together and cook for a couple of minutes.
  7. Add the chicken broth or stock to the pot and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  8. Gradually pour the dissolved cornstarch (cornflour) into the soup while stirring continuously. This will help thicken the soup.
  9. Slowly pour the beaten eggs into the soup while stirring gently. This will create thin strands of cooked egg in the soup.
  10. Taste the soup and adjust the seasoning according to your preference.
  11. Once the soup reaches the desired consistency, remove it from the heat.
  12. Serve hot, garnished with chopped spring onions

Enjoy your delicious and flavorful Chicken Manchow Soup!
